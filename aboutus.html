FarmHouse.ng

FarmHouse is the brainchild of startup founders John Dada and Kemi Sokefun. It started as an inspiration from their FrontEnd Development training. They saw a need to support a small fruit seller, Fruit Basket By Esther, with their expertise in web development, and expanded the idea into FarmHouse. Instead of supporting just one fruit seller, why not several or many more sellers? Instead of just fruits, why not entire farm produce. Today, FarmHouse serves several localities with their daily, weekly and at times monthly needs of healthy, quality and reliable farm produce, and continues to expand across the sub-saharan Africa.
